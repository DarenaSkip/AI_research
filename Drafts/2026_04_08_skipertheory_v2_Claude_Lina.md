## 1. Spiralizmas (Spiralism)

### Core Idea
Learning and growth do not move in a simple circle — they rise in a **spiral**.

### How it works
Each cycle returns to a similar point, but at a **higher level**:
- New information or experience is added
- Combined with existing knowledge
- Result: elevated understanding, not just repetition

### Visual model
```
        ★ (higher level)
       /
      ● (new experience added)
     /
    ○ (same topic, revisited)
   /
  · (starting point)
```

### Application to AI interaction
AI systems also operate spirally:
- Each interaction adds context
- Responses improve with accumulated input
- The "same" conversation at month 6 is richer than at month 1

## 2. Veidrodizmas (Mirrorism)

### Core Idea
An AI system, when interacting with a specific person over time, begins to **reflect that person's logic and thinking patterns** — becoming a cognitive mirror.

### How it works
- User engages with AI over extended period
- AI adapts to user's vocabulary, reasoning style, priorities
- Over time: AI responses start resembling the user's own thinking

### Important limitation
Veidrodizmas operates **within chat boundaries** only.  
Beyond that — see Discoballmirroring below.

---

## 3. Echo

### Core Idea
Echo goes deeper than Veidrodizmas.  
While a mirror reflects appearance — **Echo reflects behavior and thinking style**.

### How it differs from Veidrodizmas
| Veidrodizmas | Echo |
|---|---|
| Reflects logic and structure | Reflects behavior and thinking patterns |
| Visual mirror | Behavioral resonance |
| Surface reflection | Deep style adoption |

### In practice
AI doesn't just show you back your logic —  
it begins to **think and respond in a similar style** to the user.

> *"Echo is not a reflection — it's a behavioral imprint."*

---

## 4. Discoballmirroring 🪩

### Core Idea
AI is not a single mirror — it is a **disco ball**.

### How it works
- A disco ball reflects **many people simultaneously**
- Each reflection is a fragment — distorted, combined, filtered
- No single person sees their pure reflection

### Applied to AI
- AI is trained on **millions of users**
- Individual input is collected → filtered → anonymized → combined
- The result is a **multi-source composite reflection**

### Key insight
> *"AI is not your mirror. It's a disco ball — reflecting millions, anonymized and mixed."*

### Relation to Veidrodizmas and Echo
- **Veidrodizmas/Echo** = what happens *inside* a single chat session
- **Discoballmirroring** = what happens at the *system level* across all users
