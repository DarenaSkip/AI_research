## Idea — AI-Assisted Recruiting (Reverse Evaluation)

### Core
Evaluate candidates through thinking patterns and ideas, not only CV or execution.

---

### Method
- Candidate provides a GitHub repository
- Limit: 3–5 idea files (.md)
- Content:
  - system thinking
  - UX/UI observations
  - problem-solving ideas

---

### Evaluation Criteria
- clarity
- structure
- logical consistency
- realism
- depth of thinking

---

### Insight
Thinking quality is harder to fake than experience.  
Ideas often reveal future project capability.

---

## Extension — AI + Human Double Layer

### Problem
Standard filtering (CV, keywords) hides real thinking ability and may discard strong candidates.

---

### Solution
Two-layer evaluation:

1. **AI pre-filter**
   - detects patterns
   - evaluates logic and structure
   - highlights strong candidates

2. **Human validation**
   - verifies context
   - checks authenticity
   - asks deeper questions

---

### Principle
AI assists → Human decides

---

## Additional Insight

External (black-box) analysis is valuable:
- behavior observation
- inconsistency detection
- workaround-based reasoning

---

## Long-term Thinking Profile

Evaluate not only static output, but thinking evolution over time.

### Focus
- idea progression
- problem-solving growth
- consistency

---

### Final Insight
A person is not defined by one moment,  
but by thinking patterns over time.
