# Controlled Chaos (SkiperChaos)

## Concept
Using chaos as a source of ideas, while making decisions from a stable core (“eye of the storm”).

## Model
- Outer layer: chaos (ideas, noise, rapid variations)
- Inner core: stability (logic, control, decision point)

## Mechanism
⚡🧠 — instant intuition and simultaneous processing working as one

## Principle
Chaos generates options, but decisions are made from the center.

## Use cases
- Debugging
- UX analysis
- Problem solving
- Real-world situations
